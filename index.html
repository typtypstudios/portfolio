About us

We are a group of six junior game developers, students of the Master’s Degree in Computer Graphics, Games, and Virtual Reality at Rey Juan Carlos University, founded in early 2026.

Brought together by our Master's program and a shared passion for video games, we are now combining our diverse individual talents to bring to life our debut project: Mechanocult.

Mechanoccult

Duelo de mecanografía arcana y rituales absurdos.

Mechanoccult is a competitive 1v1 typing game with deck-building mechanics. Play as a cultist attempting to summon your deity before your opponent.

The core gameplay blends the agility of typing games with the tactical depth of a deck-builder. As you recite the ritual to summon your deity, you must manage your corruption level and cast strategic spells to sabotage your rival, alter their text, or protect yourself from their attacks in real time.

  • Genre: Competitive Typing, Deck-Builder, Indie
  • Release date: May 10, 2026
  • Platform: PC
  • Engine: Unity 6.3

Galaxy Trotter

A cosmic mix of management and story

Galaxy Trotter is a narrative-driven management game set in outer space. Step into the shoes of an intergalactic bartender and prepare strange drinks following your boss's recipes. It blends the management style of games like Cooking Mama and Papers, Please with a deep cosmic story.
